Currently, I am in my second year of studies in Creative Media and Game Technologies at Saxion University of Applied Sciences in Enschede. I am extremely interested in creating and designing games. I have passed my first year with no insufficient grades. I passed my propaedeutic year with all 60 ECs and an average grade of 8,0/10.

Ever since I played my first video game; New Super Mario Bros. on the DS, I knew that this was what I wanted to make when I grew up. I never gave up this dream and am still holding on to it.

Making a game is about creating a lively world, special characters and getting color and lighting right. I want to do this myself and especially focus on making stages and levels.

I created this website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, so this immediately functions as a portfolio item itself. I will use photos and screenshots to give a good impression of my interests, skills, and talents. I also hope that it gives you an idea of my passion for art and Game Design.
